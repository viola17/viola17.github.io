  • Time Series Forecasting of Electricity Loading in Cape Town Jiamin Zhong

    • Multi-Dwelling Units (MDUs) are housing estates, such as apartment complexes, consisting of individual residences. Electricity use is monitored by a bulk meter which measures the total load for the entire MDU. The bulk meter records average power readings over 30 minute intervals which are used for billing and analysis of usage patterns. This project aims at surveying through predictions models, based on bulk meter readings and weather info between 10.14.2018 and 10.13.2019, that accurately forecast future bulk usage. Refer to the jupyter notebook for the model training process and analysis. (datasets)

  • Comparison of Campus Crime with Crime in the Surrounding City. Yizhi Fang, Matt Welch, Jiamin Zhong, Jonathan Pickett

    • Can a "dangerous" city actually provide safe study enviroment? Are "rich" cities safer? To answer these questions, we used FBI arrest dataset, median income by state dataset and campus crime dataset to analyze the relations between safety level of each campus to that of its surrounding cities. First, we looked into the crime data in the cities surrounding campuses and compared that with on campus weapon, drug, alcohol violations across the States in 2010. Next, We explored how crime rates, adjusted on a population level, relate to the median income levels of each state. Check our code to see how we merged crime datasets by State and City and printed the least safe universities and summed the data on a state level to merge with the median income data by Sate. Click the report for our final conclusions.
